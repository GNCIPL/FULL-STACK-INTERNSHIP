BJP Returns to Power in Delhi After 27 Years
In the 2025 Delhi Legislative Assembly elections, the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) secured a significant victory, winning 48 out of 70 seats. Rekha Gupta has been appointed as the new Chief Minister.
